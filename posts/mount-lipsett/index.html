Mount Lipsett

June 27, 2021 · 57 words · 1 minute read hiking Canada

I was lucky enough to hike Mount Lipsett on one of those perfect summer days. It was fantastic and I’m glad I found another great hike that is gentle on the knees without sacrificing any views. It also wasn’t very busy, and bonus - absolutely no exposure or sketchy bits.
