Nova Scotia

Aug 10, 2019 · 54 words · 1 minute read travel Canada Nova Scotia

Summer 2019 - I visited the east coast of Canada for the first time. Got some aggressive wetsuit tan lines. Also tried sailing, kayaking, canoeing, boogie boarding, and windsurfing for the first time. Collected lots of periwinkle shells. Watched the tides rise and fall every day. Ate a whole lobster.

Dates: 19-08-10 to 19-09-02
