Moose Mountain

Aug 26, 2017 · 172 words · 1 minute read hiking Canada

A casual hike right outside of Calgary. I’ve been wanting to hike Moose Mountain for a while now. It was a bluebird day and even though the annual Moose Mountain Trail Race was going on, we didn’t pass by many people – always a plus!

Date: 2017-08-26

Location: Kananaskis

Distance: 15 km

Elevation gain: 515 m

Time: 5 hours, including a one-hour lunch break

Friends: Jonathan, Michelle, Nic, Emily, Connor

The hike started off with a long walk on a fire road through the forest. Afterwards, we reached a meadow which may or may not have been where Brokeback Mountain was filmed.
