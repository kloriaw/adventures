Powderface Ridge II

May 30, 2021 · 165 words · 1 minute read hiking Canada

It was easy going to the east ridge, then a lot of slow post holing through crusty snow to the north ridge. We encountered some couples taking wedding photos at the summit. The north summit was totally dry and way nicer than the east ridge. I wished we could have kept going down via the pleasant looking north slopes. Next time, I will have to try approaching this hike from the other direction.

Date: 2021-05-30

Location: Kananaskis
