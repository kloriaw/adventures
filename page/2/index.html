    Ptarmigan Cirque

    A short and popular trail that also happened to be really pretty. Drove an hour and a half to Highwood Pass, the highest paved pass in Canada. It was 30 degrees in Calgary but felt about 17 up in the cirque because of the high elevation. Date: 2017-07-25 Location: Kananaskis Distance: 6.5 km (started scrambling up Mount Rae) Elevation gain: 300 m Time: 2 hours (including long-ish breaks) Friends: Chris, Mario
