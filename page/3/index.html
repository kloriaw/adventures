  • June 30
    Table Mountain, Road trip through Castle and Waterton

    Canada Day road trip through Castle and Waterton park. Date: 2018-06-30 to 2018-07-02 Location: Castle Provincal Park, Waterton National Park Friends: Chris, and a caravan along the way Drive to Castle We started off with the most scenic drive down Highway 533, then Highway 22 - also known as Cowboy Trail. I don’t think this highway has ever disappointed me with its views. Castle Falls was a gorgeous and quiet campground, filled with the rushing sound of water from the nearby Castle Falls.
  • May 26
    Wasootch Ridge

    After walking down a riverbed for ten minutes, we realized that we were heading down the wrong trail and had to turn back to the parking lot. Despite the great weather and open views, this hike didn’t do it for me. Maybe it was the endless, unsatisfying ups and downs of the ridgewalk, or the unchanging views which got old after a few hours. We did not make it very far down the ridge before we turned back.
