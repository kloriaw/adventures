    Ha Ling Peak and Miner’s Peak

    Short and super-popular. Steep going up with some scrambling at the top. Date: 2017-08-19 Location: Kananaskis Distance: 7 km Elevation gain: 740 m Time: 4 h, including some long breaks Friends: Mario, Chris No photos from the initial ascent, which was on a well-marked trail through forest. Shortly after clearing the treeline, it gets a lot rockier and steeper. Miner’s Peak is right beside Ha Ling Peak. All these people taking different routes to the summit.
  • July 25
    Ptarmigan Cirque

    A short and popular trail that also happened to be really pretty. Drove an hour and a half to Highwood Pass, the highest paved pass in Canada. It was 30 degrees in Calgary but felt about 17 up in the cirque because of the high elevation. Date: 2017-07-25 Location: Kananaskis Distance: 6.5 km (started scrambling up Mount Rae) Elevation gain: 300 m Time: 2 hours (including long-ish breaks) Friends: Chris, Mario
